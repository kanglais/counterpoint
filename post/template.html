The Big Green Smoothie Monster

by Claire

Posted on January 1, 2016 at 10:00 AM

I'm sure that many of your are, right now, regretting that fourth glass of champagne from last night, while promising yourself that you'll have a healthy and happy 2016. At least, I hope I'm not the only one!

While many of the healthy diet fads come and go, I've found that breakfast smoothies are pretty stable in their popularity (at least, if you know how to make them right!). Below, I've included my perfect template for creating amazing smoothies- all you have to do is fill in with your favorites!

Ingredients:

  • Two Handfuls Leafy Greens (Kale, Spinach, Arugala, etc.)
  • 1/2 to 1 Cup Liquid (Almond Milk, Coconut Water, Etc.)
  • 1/2-1 Cup Frozen Fruit (Berries, Mango, Pineapple)
  • 1/2-1 Cup Alternate Frozen Fruit (Kick the flavor up a notch!)
  • 1/2 Frozen Banana or Other Sweetener (Dates, figs, or simply a few drops of Stevia are always great!)
  • 1 Scoop Protein Powder
  • Any other add-ins (Cocoa Powder, Cinnamon, Goji Berries, Chia Seeds, Oh My!)

Instructions:

Layer all ingredients in your blender, beginning with the greens, then the fruit, then the liquid. If you add powders or seeds, add them before adding in the liquid. Then, blend until it's smooth, and enjoy!

Tips for Choosing Flavors: I like my smoothies to match my favorite desserts, so I'm partial to Chocolate Cherry (frozen cherries, frozen bananas, and almond milk) or even Pumpkin Pie around the holidays (pureed pumpkin, frozen bananas and tons of spicces!). Above all, don't be afraid to experiment with new flavor combos- you might find something you love!
