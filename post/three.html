Christmas Morning Gingerbread Pancakes

by Claire

Posted on January 1, 2016 at 10:00 AM

Pancakes are, without a doubt, the best way to start any day, whether it's a weekend, a holiday, or just your average Monday. To spice things up, this Christmas we're making Gingerbread Pancakes!

These are easy and fast to make, using stuff you've already got lying around. What could be better? Nothing!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Tsp Ginger
  • 1/4 Tsp Clovesli>
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 3 Tbsp Molasses
  • 3 Tbsp Coconut Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Almond Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Coconut Oil

Instructions:

Mix together all dry ingredients till fully combined. In another bowl, whisk together all wet ingredient (molasses included). Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients, and mix. It's okay if the batter is lumpy.

Heat a skillet over medium-low, and add oil to pan (coconut works well, as does olive). Pour batter onto pan (I use a 1/4 cup, and it turns out nice-sized cakes). When bubbles in cake begin to pop and batter looks more solid, flip. Cake is done when it's golden brown on both sides.

Serve warm with butter and maple syrup, and a healthy dose of Christmas cheer!
