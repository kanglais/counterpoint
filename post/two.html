Easy Roasted Sweet Potatoes

by Claire

Posted on January 5, 2016 at 4:31 PM

One of my favorite things to eat once the temperature starts to drop is sweet potatoes. I don't care how they're cooked, whether they're savory or sweet- I just want as many as I can possibly handle!

Sweet potatoes, unlike many of their starchy vegetable counterparts, are super healthy for you. They're packed full of vitamins and minerals, plus, when they're roasted to perfection, they taste like candy.

What could be better?!

Ingredients:

  • Four medium sized sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • Butter or Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • 1 tbsp each either sweet or savory spices: Paprika, thyme, and rosemary all work for savory dishes, while cinnamon, nutmeg and allspice are excellent for sweet dishes.

Instructions:

Preheat over to 450°. In a large bowl, combine butter or oil, chopped potatoes, and spices. Mix until potatoes are evenly coated.

Spread potatoes into roasting dish in an even layer (one layer is best). Roast until tender and slightly browned, around 40 minutes. If you're feeling fancy, garnish with additional spices, dried cranberries, nuts, or seeds.
